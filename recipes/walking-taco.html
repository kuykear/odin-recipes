Walking Tacos

Walking Tacos are a delicious twist on Taco Night! The best part about Walking Tacos is that everyone can customize their meal the way they like. We've had Walking Tacos as the main meal at a birthday party and it was a hit! Everyone really enjoyed selecting their favorite mini bag of chips as the base. We got creative with the variety of chips; we had Nacho Doritos, Cool Ranch Doritios, Chili-Cheese Fritos and original Fritos. Next, it's important to have a variety of toppings. We had the basics of iceburg lettuce, tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream and black olives. But the possibilities really are endless! You could include a variety of salsas and quesos with different heats; mild, spicy or hot! Guacamole, chopped avocados, chopped jalepenos or chopped green onions are also great toppings to offer.

A few great sides to include on your Walking Taco night are:

  • Refried beans
  • Cilantro Lime Rice
  • Rice and beans (White, Brown or Yellow rice with Black or Pinto Beans)
  • Elote Corn Coleslaw
  • Mango Slaw or Salsa
  • Rotel & Velveeta Cheese dip with tortilla chips.

Required Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Ground beef (substitute with ground chicken, turkey, or meatless substitute)
  • 1 packet Taco seasoning
  • 1 tub Sour Cream
  • Mini bags of chips: Nacho or Cool Ranch Doritos, Original or Chili Cheese Fritos.
  • 1/2 head chopped Iceburg lettuce
  • 4-6 Chopped tomatoes
  • 2 lb bag Shredded Cheddar or Mexican-blend cheese or 1 block of cheese; you will shred.
  • 1 can medium Black olives

Optional Ingredients:

  • Onion
  • Nacho or White Queso

Steps

  1. Brown the ground meat in a pan on the stove. After brown, remove grease from pan. Add seasoning packet per directions on packet.
    • If you are having a party, you can keep the meat mixture warm in the slow cooker.
    • Optional: Sautee a sweet yellow onion before adding the ground meat to the pan.
  2. Prepare the chopped toppings.
    • Chop tomatoes
    • Chop lettuce
    • Slice black olives
    • Shred cheese if using a block
  3. Place toppings in a serving dish.
  4. Place sour cream in a bowl with serving spoon.
  5. Place quesos and/ or salsa out in bowls with serving spoons.
  6. Let everyone assemble their own bag of chips with toppings!
